About

WhatWeOwe is a platform that looks at what debt African governments are in and what it means to the citizens of those countries.

How It Works

Using historical data sourced from the World Bank on domestic and foreign public debt, we come up with a projection of what debt position it could be at.

Get Involved

We are always looking to make this better. All the data and code is open source. Check it out and let us know if we missed anything. Is your country not here or has outdated figures? Drop us a line. Also make sure to check out our Awesome Contributors list.
